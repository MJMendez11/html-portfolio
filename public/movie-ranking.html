The Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Catch Me If You Can

This is my favourite film. Tom Hanks and Leonardo Di Caprio are both fantastic and it leaves you feeling a certain way at the end.

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie, really changes our thoughts and perception of a possible future!

The Matrix

A film that changed how we think about computers and the world! Keanu Reeves is also one of my favourite actors!
